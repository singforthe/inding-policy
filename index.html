About Our App

INDING is a mobile application dedicated to providing accurate and unbiased information about Korean beauty products. We help users discover and explore carefully curated K-beauty products that match their specific skin type and needs.

We accept no product advertisements to ensure the accuracy and impartiality of the information we provide. We dedicate significant time and effort to identifying products suitable for each specific skin type, helping you save time in your search for healthy cosmetics.

To keep this app free and ad-free, we rely on your generous support. If you find our work helpful, please consider making a contribution—no matter how small. Your donation fuels our mission to provide the best skincare guidance. We truly appreciate your support!

Your support keeps us going.

Data Collection

We collect the following data to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal information (name, email, user ID)
  • Optional information (photos, birthday, user-generated content) - You can choose not to provide this information
  • App performance data (crash logs, diagnostics)
  • App activity data (interactions, device IDs)

We do not share your data with third parties. All data is transmitted over secure connections. For more information about how we handle your data, please read our Privacy Policy.
