How to Delete Your INDING Account

Account Deletion Steps

  1. Launch the INDING app
  2. Go to your Profile
  3. Tap the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right corner
  4. Tap 'Delete Account'

⚠️ Important Notice

  • Account deletion will permanently remove all your data
  • This action cannot be undone
  • All linked social accounts will be disconnected
  • Your data will be permanently deleted from our servers
